Your local

Farm Bureau

Advocacy Policy is changing constantly, and it has a direct impact on farmers and ranchers. Having a voice – a seat at the table and an impact on policy – is critical. Beginning at the grassroots level and involving Farm Bureau members’ advocacy efforts across the country, all of agriculture speaks with one voice through the American Farm Bureau Federation.

Meetings We have meetings on the second tuesday of every month.

Legislative Resolutions commitee meeting We Attend the Legislative Resolutions Commitee meeting each year in October.

State Farm Bureau Meeting We send representitives to the State Farm Bureau Meeting every year in the first week of November.

West Virginia Farm Lobbists We go out to support the West Virginia Farm Lobbists whenever we can.
